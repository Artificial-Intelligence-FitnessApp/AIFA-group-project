What is MAAHS?

MAAHS is a mix of a number of measurements that are taken from your body. These are...

  1. • The measurements of your skin-fold.
  2. • BMI - Body Mass Index.
  3. • MMAV - Muscle Mass Absolute Values.
  4. • The percentage of fat and lean area of your segments of the arm, thigh and leg.
  5. • The muscle circumference of your arm.

Why do I have to fill out the "Fitness Form" more than once?

You have to fill up the form twice or more times because AIFA depends on that information to help you achieve your goals. You can keep using your old WRP without update, nevertheless you won’t have much difference on your performance due to your body and your brain gets used with old WRP.
